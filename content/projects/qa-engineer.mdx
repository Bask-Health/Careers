Now Accepting Applicants for Summer '23 Quality Assurance Paid Internship

## About Us:


Bask Health is at the forefront of the health-tech industry, providing personalized healthcare experiences through advanced, user-friendly technology. Our platform serves as a launchpad for entrepreneurs, doctors, physicians, and influencers in the DTC telehealth sector.


### Job Description:

As a Quality Assurance (QA) intern at Bask, you will work directly with our QA Manager and the rest of the team to ensure our innovative telehealth platform delivers a seamless and error-free experience to our users.

We are looking to expand our team, seeking those with a keen eye for detail and a passion for delivering high-quality software products in the health-tech industry.

Our platform is designed to provide entrepreneurs, doctors, physicians, and influencers a platform to launch their own DTC telehealth brand. Bask Health is a software company in the health-tech industry, dedicated to revolutionizing the way people manage their health and wellness through advanced, user-friendly technology. Our mission is to provide accessible and personalized healthcare experiences powered by software solutions.

We are looking for someone to assist our QA team in various roles over the summer.

### What You'll Do:

- Assist in the design and execution of test plans and test cases.
- Perform functional, regression, and performance testing.
- Identify, document, and report bugs, errors, and inconsistencies in the software.
- Work closely with the development team to understand product changes and their impact on the user experience.
- Help design and run QA meetings, contributing to a culture that focuses on delivering high-quality software.
- Participate in the troubleshooting and triaging of issues with different teams to drive towards root cause identification and resolution.
- Support the coordination and execution of user acceptance testing.
- Participate in brainstorming sessions and contribute innovative ideas for improving testing processes and strategies.
- Gain exposure to various aspects of a fast-growing startup, including product development, customer support, and sales and marketing.

### Who We're Looking For:

- We're looking for someone who is passionate about software quality and is excited about transforming the world of health tech. If you're eager to learn and help ensure our platform delivers an exceptional user experience, we'd love to hear from you.
- You have excellent attention to detail and problem-solving skills.
- You are comfortable working in a fast-paced, unstructured, startup environment.
- You have the ability to work independently and collaborate effectively as part of a team.
- Previous coursework or experience in software testing, computer science, or a related field is preferred, but not required.

We are a highly collaborative team looking for a QA intern who is ready to learn and grow with us. This is a unique opportunity to help shape the future of telehealth. Apply now to join our mission!
