Now Accepting Applicants for Summer '23 Paid Sales and Marketing Co-Op/Internship

## About Us:


Bask Health is at the forefront of the health-tech industry, providing personalized healthcare experiences through advanced, user-friendly technology. Our platform serves as a launchpad for entrepreneurs, doctors, physicians, and influencers in the DTC telehealth sector.


### Job Description:

We are looking to expand our team, seeking those with a deep-rooted entrepreneurial spirit and a passion for healthcare technology.


Our platform is designed to provide entrepreneurs, doctors, physicians, and influencers a platform to launch their own DTC telehealth brand. Bask Health is a software company in the health-tech industry, dedicated to revolutionizing the way people manage their health and wellness through advanced, user-friendly technology. Our mission is to provide accessible and personalized healthcare experiences powered by software solutions.


We are looking for someone to assist our sales and marketing team in various roles over the summer.

### What You'll Do:


- Assist in the development and execution of marketing strategies, including SEO optimization and social media campaigns.
- Collaborate with the sales team to understand customer needs and identify strategies to meet sales goals.
- Help design and run sales and marketing meetings, contributing to a culture that focuses on delivering high-impact experiences.
- Assist in the creation of engaging content for our website and other marketing channels.
- Support the development of creative content that aligns with Bask Health's brand and messaging, which includes blogs, social media posts, newsletters, video scripts, and website content.
- Participate in market research to keep abreast of the latest trends in the telehealth industry.
- Support the coordination and execution of social media campaigns.
- Participate in brainstorming sessions and contribute innovative ideas for content and marketing strategies.
- Gain exposure to various aspects of a fast-growing startup, including sales, marketing, and product development.

### Who We're Looking For:

We're looking for someone who's entrepreneurial and willing to roll up their sleeves to get the work done. 

You're passionate about our mission to disrupt the healthcare industry, and are eager to learn and create impactful marketing strategies. If you're excited about transforming the world of health tech, we'd love to hear from you.

- You have a strong understanding of social media platforms (Instagram, Twitter, TikTok, etc.) and content management systems
- You possess excellent written and verbal communication skills; you are a strong communicator - able to clearly articulate marketing plans, constraints and timelines.
- You are comfortable working in a fast-paced, unstructured, startup environment.
- You have the ability to work independently and collaborate effectively as part of a team.
- Previous experience in sales, customer service, marketing, or a related field is preferred, but not required.

We are a highly collaborative team looking for a sales and marketing intern who is ready to learn and grow with us. 

This is a unique opportunity to help shape the future of telehealth. Apply now to join our mission!
